# 30x30 — Marketing & Content Playbook

## The Hook

**"I'm building 30 things in 30 days. No plan. No excuses. Day 1 starts now."**

That's it. That's the launch post. Everything else is documentation of the journey.

---

## Rik's Personal 30x30 Challenge

Rik goes first. Always. The creator doing the challenge is the proof that it works.

**The format:**
- One build per day, made on the fly to solve a real problem
- Documented as it happens — no polish, no retakes
- Posted across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts daily
- Every video ends with: "Join the challenge at 30x30.midcurved.com"

**Content structure per day (DCR):**
- **Disruption:** "Most people think you need weeks to build a tool. I did it in 47 minutes."
- **Conflict:** Show the problem, the confusion, the iteration
- **Recognition:** The shipped thing. The lesson. The link.

**This is the content engine.** 30 days = 30 videos = 30 pieces of proof.

---

## Channel Strategy

### Instagram / TikTok (daily)
- 60-90 second screen recordings with voiceover
- Raw, fast, no intro
- Caption: what I built + link in bio
- Hashtags: #30x30 #vibecoding #buildinpublic #aitools

### YouTube Shorts (daily)
- Same content as IG/TikTok, repurposed
- Build library of searchable "I built X in 1 day" content

### YouTube Long-form (weekly)
- Week recap: best 3 builds, lessons, what flopped
- "I built 7 things this week. Here's what actually worked."

### X / Twitter (daily)
- One tweetable insight per build
- Example: "Day 12: built a quiz that generates challenge ideas with Claude API. 200 lines of HTML. Live in 2 hours. The moat isn't the idea — it's the shipping speed."

### Telegram (ongoing)
- Free community, open to all participants
- Daily "what did you ship today?" thread
- Best builds get reshared on Midcurved

## Launch Sequence

**Week -1 (pre-launch teaser):**
- Post: "I'm doing something stupid. Building 30 things in 30 days. Starting [date]. Details soon."
- Story poll: "Could you build 1 thing per day for 30 days?"
- Build hype, capture early interest

**Day 0 (launch):**
- Drop the tool: "I built an AI idea generator so you have zero excuses. Go to 30x30.midcurved.com"
- Post your Day 1 build
- DM 20 builder friends personally

**Days 1-30:**
- Daily content, no exceptions
- Repost best submissions from community
- Feature a participant build every 3-4 days

**Day 30:**
- "I shipped 30 things in 30 days. Here's everything." — long-form recap
- Announce next cohort

## "No Excuses" Messaging

The challenge is built around eliminating every excuse:

| Excuse | Answer |
|--------|--------|
| "I don't know what to build" | The idea generator figures it out for you |
| "30 ideas is too many" | AI generates your full 30-day plan |
| "I don't have time" | Quiz asks how much time you have. 30 min/day counts. |
| "I don't know how to code" | Apps, videos, designs, automations — all valid |
| "I'll start next month" | Rolling challenge. Start today. |
| "No one will care" | vibecode.fun is the audience. We're watching. |

Use these in content. Directly. Name the excuses, kill them one by one.

## Creator Partnerships

Target accounts in the builder / AI / no-code space:

- Reach out with: "I'm doing 30x30 and documenting every build. Want to co-promote? I'll feature your audience in the challenge."
- Offer: cross-post, shoutout, feature their community's builds
- Target follower range: 5k-50k (same tier, real engagement)

- vibecode.fun submissions with #30x30
