# 30x30 — Strategy, Goals & Funnel

## The Core Idea

Build 30 things in 30 days. One per day. Ship it publicly.

No fixed start date. Rolling. Start whenever. The only rule: make it, post it, move on.

The **idea generator** is the entry point. It removes the blank page problem and converts curiosity into commitment — with a paywall that filters serious people from tourists.

---

## Why This Works

Most challenges fail because people don't start. They overthink the format, can't commit to 30 ideas upfront, and have no accountability. 30x30 solves all three:

- **Blank page** → idea generator does the thinking
- **30 ideas** → AI generates a personalized roadmap
- **No accountability** → Telegram community + public submissions on vibecode.fun
- **No commitment** → rolling start, no cohort pressure

The €9 paywall isn't about revenue. It's a filter. People who pay €9 are 10x more likely to actually do the thing.
